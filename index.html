Vinland is the new multiplayer online role-playing game (MMORPG). Players control a character avatar within a game world in third- or first-person view, exploring the landscape, fighting various monsters, completing quests, interacting with non-player characters (NPCs) or other players.

Our goal is to bring value to the gaming industry by combining NFT and MMORPG in one platform giving value to the in game produces, weapons and characters.

Tournaments

Create or join matches and tournaments, find an opponent in no time and play for NFT, and token prizes. Little to no involvement is required for organization, we do everything so you can play tons of matches every day!

Develop your skills

As characters become more developed, they gain various talents and skills, requiring the player to further define the abilities of that character. Characters can choose two primary professions that can focus on producing items, such as tailoring, blacksmithing or jewelcrafting or on gathering from resource nodes, such as skinning or mining. Characters can use there talents to create their in game NFTs and sell and trade them on our platform for VIN tokens.
